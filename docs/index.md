# Welcome to BatCast

The UF Bat House complex is the largest human-made bat roost in the world. We're working to better understand and protect the bats that live there, as well as to better predict their behavior. By collecting data on how many bats emerge on a given night, we will build a model of when bats are most active and forecast how many emerge on a given evening. The emergence of these bats is a spectacular display, with up to 500,000 emerging in a swirling stream on warm evenings!

This project represents an ecological modeling and outreach initiative by researchers in the Department of Biology at UF.
